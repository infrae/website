Silva Extensions and enhancements

Atom/RSS feed

Subscribe to a RSS or Atom feed of the Silva Extensions packages.

On this page you can download extensions from the Silva community. The extensions can be used to add new functionality to Silva (or Silva extensions themselves). Many of them are included in Silva 2.1 and later.

If you have questions or problems with one of the extensions, either send an email to the author of the extension, or better, send it to the mailinglist and there will be someone willing to answer your questions. Extensions are ordered by most recent release.
