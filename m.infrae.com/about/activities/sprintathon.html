Rotterdam Sprintathon for Zope3

These pages carry reports about the Sprintathon that was held from 2-6 december 2002. The sprint was held in Rotterdam, the Netherlands, at V2_Organisation, Institute for the Unstable Media. Read a description of (and see) the sprint environment, the people attending, and the tasks accomplished.
