Consultancy

We can’t solve problems by using the same kind of thinking we used when we created them.

—Albert Einstein, physicist, Nobel laureate (1879-1955)

Infrae’s developers are available for consultancy on a custom or regular basis. Their expertise lies primarily in the web, Python/Zope development, and Internet infrastructure areas. This includes product development; Python applications and ‘glue’ code; XML technologies; Javascript client-side applications; page templating, site layout construction, and CSS; web server performance, load balancing, and caching.

Consultancy can be advisory support for development of in-house software applications, development services where our people participate in building applications, or training for knowledge transfer.

For inquiries call or contact sales at infrae com. 

Coaching Card Product

For organizations that have intermittent and/or unpredictable support and coaching needs, Infrae offers a Coaching Card. A card contains 20 or 40 units (half man-days) and the client can request support when needed. For the client this organizes undetermined support into an overseeable structure and ensures experts are on-call.

This approach also simplifies the financial aspect of intermittent support. The Coaching Card is – like the ‘strippenkaart’ used for public transportation in the Netherlands – paid in advance and valid for a year.

For inquiries call or contact sales at infrae com.
