Training

Success is getting what you want. Happiness is wanting what you get.

—Dale Carnegie

Infrae offers training for its own products, and also provides general training in certain technologies, such as Zope3 and the Python programming language. Trainings are almost always a customized service where we work out the subject matter together with the customer.

We have one day, two day, and four day trainings available for Silva, Kupu, Zope3, and Python programming. These trainings can also be organized with our Coaching Card product.

For inquiries call or contact info at infrae com.
