European Network

Infrae has frequent contact with the parties listed. All of them are involved in open source software development. Some – such as OpenWeb Solution, POV, gocept, Pilot Systems, Bubblenet, OpenApp, and Zea Partners – can provide services and consulting for Infrae software.

This is a partial listing of only European companies, and doesn’t include numerous independent contractors available in the network and around the globe.

Business

Infrae is a founding partner of the ZEAPartners business network.

ZEAP is a non-profit association created in 2003. Sponsor companies pay an annual fee and receive benefits in three areas: marketing, organization, and network building.

Community

Infrae is a Solution Provider member of the Zope Foundation.

The Zope Foundation is the copyright holder of the Zope platform and many extensions and associated software.
