Route description

By train and foot

The Infrae office is located behind the train station Rotterdam Centraal. When you arrive in the station, walk to the back entrance (the track numbers will go up). Keep walking straight out of the station, along the left side of the canal (called the Spoorsingel). The third street on your left is Hoevestraat, our building is number 10. Ring the bell for ‘Kantoor’.
